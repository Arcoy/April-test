### An April Unit has
3 identifers: Level, Name, and Class

7 visible stats: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Wisdom, Intelegence, Charisma, Skill

3 "invisible stats": Hit Rate, Avoid Rate

It also has a carry weight and Inventory

### Invisible Stats:
#### Hit Rate:
skill + (2 * level)
#### Avoid:
Dex + (2 *level)
#### Max Hit Points
con + (2 * level)
